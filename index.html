Welcome to Sales Club

Sales is found in any career path. The Sales Club is a club designed to give students of any major a leg up on gaining real-world knowledge and career opportunities in sales. Students participating in Sales Club can expect to have opportunities at their disposal such as sales competitions, networking events, leadership opportunities, interview preparation, and community involvement. Our mission statement is to connect students to sales careers. Given that 75% of business school graduates find themselves in customer-facing roles post-graduation, the Sales Club is providing key resources to keep Bulldogs ahead of the competition. We are not just a club, we are the bridge from college to career for students, run by students. *In accordance with University of Minnesota Policy, all students are welcome to join and participate in this group's activities as members or supporters, regardless of identity*

Who We Are

Sales Club is a student-led organization focused on developing strong communication, relationship-building, and professional skills. We connect members with real-world sales experiences, guest speakers, and practical workshops so they can build confidence in any business setting.

What We Offer

Sales Training

Hands-on workshops help members build prospecting techniques, pitch development, and consultative selling skills. Learn how to present with confidence and close deals in competitive scenarios.

Networking & Career Growth

Connect with professionals, alumni, and recruiters through club events, guest speaker sessions, and career panels. Explore internship and job opportunities while growing your professional network.
