About Sales Club

Only 2% of Universities nationwide offer an officially recognized Sales Program. As of 2019, the University of Minnesota Duluth has joined the exclusive list of colleges offering this invaluable major. Within the first semester of its launch, the Professional Sales Major ranks as one of the largest majors in the Labovitz School of Business and Economics. Over the course of the major's existence, many Bulldogs have continued to grow outside of their comfort zone in their professional careers.

Our Mission

Sales is found in any career path. The Sales Club is a club designed to give students of any major a leg up on gaining real-world knowledge and career opportunities in sales. Students participating in Sales Club can expect to have opportunities at their disposal such as sales competitions, networking events, leadership opportunities, interview preparation, and community involvement. Our mission statement is to connect students to sales careers. Given that 75% of business school graduates find themselves in customer-facing roles post-graduation, the Sales Club is providing key resources to keep Bulldogs ahead of the competition. We are not just a club, we are the bridge from college to career for students, run by students. *In accordance with University of Minnesota Policy, all students are welcome to join and participate in this group's activities as members or supporters, regardless of identity*

Mentorship Program

The UMD Sales Club Mentorship Program bridges the experience gap through professional connections by matching underclassmen with upperclassmen mentors.

The program fosters meaningful personal and professional relationships while building stronger bonds between new and veteran members. Mentors provide career guidance, interview coaching, resume support, and help mentees set goals for sales, business, and even student leadership on the basketball team.
